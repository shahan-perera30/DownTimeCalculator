Other costs of downtime:

  • Overtime: Additional costs if downtime is replaced with overtime.
  • Additional inventories: Higher inventories due to uncertainty in machine availability.
  • Maintenance labor: Extra personnel needed to fix machines.
  • Loss of revenue: Lost sales when orders are not fulfilled.
  • Additional repair costs: For example, expedited shipping for spare parts.
